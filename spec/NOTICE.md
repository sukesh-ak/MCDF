## Exclusions

Patent Exclusion Notices made against an MCDF Draft Deliverable or Approved
Deliverable, per the Community Specification License. Each notice MUST include:

- Name of party making the Exclusion Notice:
- Name of patent owner:
- Specification:
- Version number:

For issued patents / published applications: the patent number(s) or title and
application number(s), and identification of the specific part(s) of the
Specification whose implementation makes the excluded claim a Necessary Claim.
For unpublished applications: the text of the filed application, or identification
of the implicated part(s) of the Specification.
